## What is SEF® Lean Version?

The **Secure Enterprise Framework® (SEF®) Lean Version** is a structured Enterprise Architecture Framework (EAF) designed to assist organisations manage complexity in an efficient and effective manner. It can be applied across entire organisations or within large-scale transformation programmes.

The SEF® Lean Version addresses the challenges associated with organisational and programme-level transformation, defines the core components of the framework, and outlines the business benefits of its adoption.

The framework is intended for enterprise architects, service architects, business architects, information architects, infrastructure architects, integration architects, security architects, programme managers, and business directors.
